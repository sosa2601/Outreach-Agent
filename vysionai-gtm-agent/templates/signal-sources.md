## Primary Signal Sources

These are the sources to search first. High signal-to-noise ratio for Saswati's ICP.

### 1. Job Boards (Strongest Signal)
Companies posting these roles are actively building GTM functions:

**Target roles to search for:**
- Head of Growth
- VP Sales (first or second hire)
- GTM Engineer
- Revenue Operations Manager
- Sales Operations Manager
- Marketing Operations Manager
- First Marketing Hire
- Demand Generation Manager
- SDR Manager (hiring a team = scaling outbound)

**Where to search:**
- LinkedIn Jobs: `site:linkedin.com/jobs "Head of Growth" "B2B SaaS"`
- Greenhouse: `site:greenhouse.io "RevOps" "SaaS"`
- Lever: `site:lever.co "GTM" "startup"`
- Workable: `site:apply.workable.com "marketing ops"`
- AngelList/Wellfound: `site:wellfound.com "growth" "series a"`
- Ashby: `site:jobs.ashbyhq.com "revenue operations"`

**Search query templates:**
```
"Head of Growth" "B2B SaaS" job 2026 site:linkedin.com
"first marketing hire" B2B startup hiring 2026
"RevOps" "no existing system" OR "from scratch" job posting
"GTM Engineer" SaaS startup 2026
"VP Sales" "series a" OR "series b" hiring
```

### 2. Funding Announcements (Budget Signal)
Companies that raised in last 90 days have fresh budget for tooling.

**Where to search:**
- TechCrunch: `site:techcrunch.com "raises" "series a" OR "series b" 2026`
- Crunchbase News: `site:news.crunchbase.com SaaS funding 2026`
- Axios Pro Rata: funding newsletters
- LinkedIn posts: founders announcing rounds themselves

**Search query templates:**
```
B2B SaaS "Series A" funding 2026 announced
GTM OR RevOps SaaS "just raised" 2026
"closed" "$" "million" B2B SaaS 2026 site:techcrunch.com
startup funding "go-to-market" OR "GTM" 2026
```

**Filter:** Look for $2M–$20M raises. Too small = no budget. Too large = enterprise buying.

---

### 3. LinkedIn (Intent Signal)
Founders and GTM leaders posting about their challenges = open to solutions.

**What to look for:**
- Founders posting about building outbound from scratch
- GTM leaders posting about RevOps pain
- CEOs posting about scaling without hiring
- Anyone posting about AI in sales/marketing

**Search queries:**
```
LinkedIn search: "building outbound" "from scratch" B2B SaaS 2026
LinkedIn search: "no RevOps" OR "first RevOps" B2B startup
LinkedIn search: "AI automation" GTM OR sales operations
LinkedIn search: "outbound" "not working" OR "broken" B2B
```

---

### 4. G2/Capterra Reviews (Pain Signal)
Companies that leave detailed reviews on tools often reveal their GTM gaps.

**What to look for:**
- Reviews mentioning "wished it integrated with X"
- Reviews about manual workarounds
- Reviews about needing better automation

**Search queries:**
```
G2 reviews "HubSpot" "manual" OR "no automation" 2025 2026
Capterra "Apollo" "wish it could" OR "limitations" B2B
G2 "Smartlead" reviews recent 2026
```

---

### 5. Product Hunt (Launch Signal)
New SaaS launches = companies with product but no GTM system yet.

**Where to search:**
- `site:producthunt.com B2B SaaS 2026`
- Filter: launched in last 90 days, B2B category

**Signal:** New product + no established GTM motion = perfect timing.

---

### 6. Twitter/X and Newsletter Mentions
Founders building in public often reveal exact GTM challenges.

**Search queries:**
```
Twitter: "building outbound" B2B SaaS 2026
Twitter: "RevOps" "struggling" OR "help" startup
Substack/Newsletter: GTM SaaS founders 2026
```

## Signal Quality Rating

Not all signals are equal. Rate signals before passing to company-research:

| Signal Type | Quality | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Hiring GTM role (RevOps, Growth, SDR Manager) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Direct budget signal, clear pain |
| Founder posting about GTM pain on LinkedIn | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Decision maker expressing problem publicly |
| Series A/B funding in last 60 days | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Fresh budget, growth pressure |
| New product launch on Product Hunt | ⭐⭐⭐ | Good timing, needs GTM |
| Series A/B funding 61–180 days ago | ⭐⭐⭐ | Budget still available, less urgency |
| G2 review mentioning automation pain | ⭐⭐ | Pain signal but no timing |
| Company exists and fits ICP (no trigger) | ⭐ | Weak — no urgency signal |

**Rule:** Only pass ⭐⭐⭐ or higher signals to the pipeline.
⭐⭐ and below go to a nurture/watch list, not active outreach.

## Signals to Watch and Revisit

Some companies are not ready now but will be soon.
Track these and revisit in 30–60 days:

- Company at pre-PMF stage (revisit when they announce first customers)
- Company with no hiring signal but strong ICP match (revisit when they post a GTM role)
- Company with Series A announced but no GTM hire yet (revisit in 30 days)
- Company with flat headcount and great ICP (revisit when they announce growth plans)
