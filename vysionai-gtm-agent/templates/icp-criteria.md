## Who VysionAI Serves

VysionAI builds AI automation and GTM systems for B2B SaaS companies
that have revenue and product-market fit but are still running GTM manually.
The ideal client has a GTM pain they know exists but haven't built the system to fix it.

---

## Firmographic Criteria

| Criteria | Ideal Range | Why It Matters | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| Industry | B2B SaaS, GTM tech, RevOps tools, AI/ML tools | Understands automation value | B2C, non-tech, hardware |
| Company Size | 10–200 employees | Small enough to move fast, big enough to have budget | <5 employees or >500 |
| Revenue Stage | $500K–$20M ARR estimated | Has paying customers, has budget for tooling | Pre-revenue or enterprise |
| Funding Stage | Bootstrapped/profitable OR Seed–Series B | Has budget, feels urgency to grow | Series C+ (too complex to sell) |
| Geography | US-based (primary), UK/AU/CA (secondary) | English-speaking GTM teams | Non-English primary market |
| Company Age | 2–8 years old | Past chaos phase, not yet fully systematised | <1 year (too early) or >15 years (too set) |
| Growth Signal | Hiring GTM/growth roles OR recently funded | Actively trying to grow | Flat headcount, no hiring, no news |

## GTM Maturity Criteria (Most Important)

The ideal company is in the "growing manually" stage:
- They have a sales or marketing function (1–5 people)
- They are doing outbound OR inbound but NOT both systematically
- They do NOT have a RevOps person yet OR just hired their first one
- They are using HubSpot or Salesforce but it is not set up properly
- Their outbound is manual: list → Apollo → Smartlead → hope
- They have no lead scoring or signal-based routing
- CEO or founder is still involved in sales (founder-selling)

**This is the gap Saswati fills.**

## Tech Stack Signals

### Signals That Indicate GOOD FIT
- Uses HubSpot (Saswati's systems integrate with it)
- Uses Apollo or ZoomInfo for prospecting
- Uses Smartlead, Instantly, or similar for sending
- Has a Webflow or modern website (tech-forward team)
- Uses Notion or Coda for documentation (operator mindset)
- Has Slack (team is async/modern)
- Uses Zapier or Make (they value automation but may need to upgrade)

### Signals That Indicate GREAT FIT (They Need an Upgrade)
- Uses Zapier but posting jobs for "marketing ops" or "RevOps" = outgrown Zapier
- Has HubSpot but no lead scoring = system is there but not working
- Has Apollo but sequences are manual = knows the tool, needs the system
- Posts jobs mentioning "build automation from scratch" = green field opportunity

### Signals That Indicate POOR FIT
- Uses Salesforce + Marketo + enterprise stack (too complex for VysionAI's approach)
- Has a dedicated 5+ person RevOps team (already built the system)
- Uses only free tools (no budget for paid tooling)
- No CRM visible (too early stage)

## Buyer Persona Profiles

### Persona 1 — The Founder/CEO (Highest Priority)
- **Company size:** 10–50 employees
- **Situation:** Still personally involved in sales. Knows they need systems. No time to build them.
- **Pain:** Every week doing manual list pulls and writing emails themselves
- **What they say:** "I need to systematise our outbound but I don't have time to learn n8n"
- **What they respond to:** Fast ROI, operator who has done it before, specific system not vague consulting
- **CTA that works:** "Would it make sense to show you the exact system?" (yes/no)

### Persona 2 — Head of Growth / VP Marketing (Primary)
- **Company size:** 30–150 employees
- **Situation:** Owns pipeline. Has a team of 1–3. Under pressure to scale without hiring.
- **Pain:** Reporting into CEO on pipeline. Manual processes are the bottleneck.
- **What they say:** "We're doing outbound but nothing is automated, it's all manual"
- **What they respond to:** Systems thinking, measurable outcomes, specific integrations
- **CTA that works:** "Is your outbound stack fully automated right now?" (yes/no)

### Persona 3 — First RevOps Hire (Emerging)
- **Company size:** 50–200 employees
- **Situation:** Just hired. Expected to build the system from scratch. Overwhelmed.
- **Pain:** Needs to show value quickly. Doesn't know where to start.
- **What they say:** "I'm the first RevOps hire here and we have nothing set up"
- **What they respond to:** Practical help, fast deployment, not another SaaS tool
- **CTA that works:** "What's the first system you're being asked to build?"

## Negative ICP — Who to Avoid

| Disqualifier | Specific Red Flag | Why It Disqualifies |
|---|---|---|
| Too small | <5 employees, no revenue signal | No budget, decision fatigue |
| Too large | 500+ employees, enterprise stack | Long procurement, complex buying |
| Consumer business | B2C product, consumer app | GTM motion completely different |
| Already built | 5+ person RevOps team, mature automation | Don't need VysionAI |
| Pre-PMF | <$100K revenue signals, pivoting frequently | Can't afford, unstable needs |
| Budget constrained | Only free tools, bootstrapped AND small | Won't pay for tooling |
| Wrong vertical | Manufacturing, retail, non-tech | Automation mindset not there |
| Enterprise stack only | Salesforce + Marketo + 6sense | VysionAI's stack won't integrate cleanly |
| No GTM function yet | No sales or marketing hire | Too early |

## Scoring Weights for Skills

When skills score companies, use these weights:

| Dimension | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ICP Firmographic Match | 25% | Size, industry, stage |
| GTM Pain Visibility | 20% | Evidence of the problem we solve |
| Budget Signals | 20% | Funding, stack, hiring |
| Tech Stack Fit | 20% | HubSpot, Apollo, modern tools |
| Outreach Readiness | 15% | Can we find the right person? |

**Grade Bands:**
- A (80–100): Hot — pursue immediately
- B (60–79): Warm — pursue with tailored approach
- C (40–59): Lukewarm — nurture only
- Skip (<40): Do not pursue

## Quick 60-Second Qualification Checklist

Answer yes or no to each:

1. Is it a B2B SaaS company with 10–200 employees? (Y/N)
2. Do they show growth signals: hiring, funding, or content in last 90 days? (Y/N)
3. Is their GTM motion manual or partially automated? (Y/N)
4. Do they use HubSpot, Apollo, or modern GTM tools? (Y/N)
5. Can I find a named decision maker to contact? (Y/N)

**5 Yes = Grade A | 3–4 Yes = Grade B | 1–2 Yes = Grade C | 0 Yes = Skip**
